# Member Management Module — ChurchCMS

## Overview

The **Member Management Module** allows administrators and staff to create, manage, and maintain member records within ChurchCMS.

It serves as the central system for:
- Member identity & profile management  
- Communication tracking  
- Group and family relationships  
- Attendance and activity history  

---

## Add a New Member

### Steps

1. Login using **Admin** or **Staff** credentials  
2. Navigate to: Users → Members
3. Click **Add** (top-right corner)  
4. Fill in required details:
- Full Name  
- Email ID  
- Phone Number  
- Aadhar Number  
- Employment ID  
- Date of Birth  
- Additional information  

5. Click **Submit**

### Result

- Member is successfully created  
- Appears in the Members list  

### Note

- Ensure the **Email ID is valid** (used for login & communication)

---

## View Member Details

### Steps

1. Go to: User->members
2. Click on a **Member Name**

### Result

- Full member profile opens

## Edit Member

### Steps

1. Open member profile  
2. Click **Edit**  
3. Update fields  
4. Click **Submit**

---

## Delete Member

### Steps

1. Open member profile  
2. Click **Delete**  
3. Confirm action  

### Result

- Member is permanently removed  

---

## Member Profile Actions

Access via: Member Profile → (⋮) Three-dot menu

---

### 1. Deactivate Member
- Marks member as inactive

### 2. Reset Password
- Resets member login password  

---

### 3. Verify Email
- Sends verification email  

---

### 4. Subscribe to Newsletter
- Updates subscription status  

---

### 5. Messaging

Supports:
- Internal Message  
- Email  
- SMS

1. Click **Messaging**  
2. Select channel
