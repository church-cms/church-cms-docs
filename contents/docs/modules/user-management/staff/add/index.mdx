# How to Add a New Staff Member

## Step-by-Step Procedure

1. Login to the system using **Admin** credentials.
2. From the left navigation pane, click **Users**.
3. Select **Staff**.

![Staff List](/modules/staff/staff-list.png)

---

## Adding a New Staff

1. Click the **Add New** button.

![Add Staff Button](/modules/staff/add-staff-button.png)

2. A new staff registration form will open.
3. Enter all the required details in the form.

![Add Staff Form](/modules/staff/add-staff-form-1.png)

![Add Staff Form](/modules/staff/add-staff-form-2.png)

4. Click **Submit**.

![Add Staff Form Submit](/modules/staff/add-staff-form-3.png)

---

## Outcome

- The new staff member will be added successfully to the portal.
- The staff record will appear in the **Staff** list.

---

## Notes

- Ensure all mandatory fields are filled before submission.
- Verify details such as contact information and role for accuracy.
