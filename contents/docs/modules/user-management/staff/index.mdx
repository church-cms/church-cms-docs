# Staff Management Module

## Introduction

The **Staff Management** module enables administrators to add and manage staff records within the system. This module helps maintain accurate staff information for operational and administrative purposes.

---

## Accessing the Staff List

Navigate to **Users → Staff** from the main sidebar.

![Staff List](/modules/staff/staff-list.png)

---

## Page Toolbar

The toolbar at the top of the Staff list provides the following actions:

| Button | Description |
|---|---|
| **Add +** | Opens the [Add Staff](/docs/modules/user-management/staff/add) form |

---

## Staff List Items

Each row in the list represents a staff member and shows key details at a glance. Click a staff member's **name** to open their [Staff Detail](/docs/modules/user-management/staff/detail) page.
