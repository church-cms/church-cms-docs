# How to Access Staff Profile

## Steps

1. Login to the system using **Admin** or **Staff** credentials.
2. Navigate to **Users → Staff**.
3. Click the **name** and the profile will be opened.

![Staff List Click](/modules/staff/staff-list-click.png)

---

## Staff Profile Sections

![Staff Profile Overview](/modules/staff/staff-profile-overview.png)

The following tabs and details can be viewed in the profile:

- My Profile
- Timeline
- Family Tree
- Family
- Messages
- Notes
- Attendance

---

## 1. Profile

Displays the complete profile information of the staff member. Includes personal details such as name, contact information, date of birth, and other registered details.

![Staff My Profile Tab](/modules/staff/staff-my-profile-tab.png)

---

## 2. Timeline

Displays the activity history of the staff profile. All updates and actions performed in the profile can be viewed here.

![Staff Timeline](/modules/staff/staff-timeline-1.png)

![Staff Timeline](/modules/staff/staff-timeline-2.png)

---

## 3. Family Tree

Displays the family details linked to the staff member. Family information such as:

- Name of the family member
- Relationship
- Date of Birth
- Email ID
- Other related details can be viewed here.

![Staff Family Tree](/modules/staff/staff-family-tree-1.png)

![Staff Family Tree](/modules/staff/staff-family-tree-2.png)

---

## 4. Family

Family tree details can be added while creating the member profile in the Users module.

![Staff Family](/modules/staff/staff-family-1.png)

![Staff Family](/modules/staff/staff-family-2.png)

---

## 5. Messages

Displays messages sent by the member. The following details can be viewed:

- Status
- Subject
- Message
- Attachments
- Sent On
- Action

![Staff Messages](/modules/staff/staff-messages-1.png)

![Staff Messages](/modules/staff/staff-messages-2.png)

---

## 6. Notes

Admin can add notes related to the member.

1. Enter the note in the provided field.
2. Click **Submit** to save the note.

![Staff Notes](/modules/staff/staff-notes-1.png)

![Staff Notes](/modules/staff/staff-notes-2.png)

---

## 7. Attendance

The attendance details of the member can be viewed in this section.

![Staff Attendance](/modules/staff/staff-attendance-1.png)

![Staff Attendance](/modules/staff/staff-attendance-2.png)

## Available Actions

Additional actions are available from the **⋮ menu** at the top-right of the profile. See [Menu Options](/docs/modules/user-management/staff/menu-options) for the full list.
