# Available Member Profile Actions

---

## Deactivate Staff

1. Click **Deactivate**.
2. A confirmation message will appear asking whether you want to change the member's status.
3. Click **OK**.
4. The member will be deactivated successfully.

![Deactivate Staff Option](/modules/staff/staff-deactivate-option.png)

![Deactivate Staff Confirm](/modules/staff/staff-deactivate-confirm.png)

---

## Reset Password

1. Click **Reset Password**.
2. A confirmation message will appear asking whether you want to reset the password.
3. Click **OK**.
4. The password will be reset successfully.

![Reset Password Option](/modules/staff/staff-reset-password-option.png)

![Reset Password Confirm](/modules/staff/staff-reset-password-confirm.png)

---

## Verify Email

1. Click **Verify Email**.
2. A verification email will be sent to the member's registered email address.

![Verify Email Option](/modules/staff/staff-verify-email-option.png)

![Verify Email Confirm](/modules/staff/staff-verify-email-confirm.png)
