# Menu Options

The **⋮ (three-dot) menu** at the top-right of a [Member Detail](/docs/modules/user-management/members/detail) page provides a set of administrative actions for that specific member.

## Accessing the Menu

1. Open a member's profile (**Users → Members → click name**).
2. Click the **⋮** icon at the top-right of the profile panel.

---

## Available Actions

### Deactivate Member

Marks the member as **inactive** without deleting their record.

- Inactive members do not appear in active member lists.
- They do not receive system communications or newsletters.
- Their record is preserved and can be reactivated at any time.

<Note type="info">
  Use **Deactivate** when a member is temporarily away or on a leave of absence.
</Note>

---

### Reactivate Member

Restores a previously deactivated member to **active** status. This option replaces "Deactivate" when viewing an inactive member's profile.

---

### Reset Password

Sends a **password reset link** to the member's registered email address, allowing them to set a new login password.

---

### Verify Email

Dispatches an **email verification link** to the member's registered email. Use this if the member's email address was added manually and has not been verified.

---

### Subscribe to Newsletter

Toggles the member's **newsletter subscription** status. Selecting this option will subscribe or unsubscribe them depending on their current state.

---

### Messaging

Send a direct message to the member via one of three supported channels:

| Channel | Description |
|---|---|
| Internal Message | In-app notification visible in the member's account |
| Email | Sent to the member's registered email address |
| SMS | Sent to the member's registered phone number |

**Steps:**
1. Select **Messaging** from the ⋮ menu.
2. Choose the desired channel (Internal / Email / SMS).
3. Enter the **Subject**, **Message Body**, and any **Attachments**.
4. Click **Send**.

---

### Add Family Member

Links a new or existing person as a family member of this member.

**Required fields:**

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| Relationship | e.g., Spouse, Child, Parent, Sibling |
| Name | Family member's full name |
| Gender | Male / Female / Other |
| Date of Birth | For records and birthday tracking |
| Mobile Number | Primary contact number |

**Optional fields:** Blood Group, Aadhar Number, Email

Click **Submit** to save. The entry will appear under the **Family** tab on the member's [Detail page](/docs/modules/user-management/members/detail#family).

---

### Generate Membership Card

Instantly generates a **printable membership card** for the member, pre-filled with their name, photo, membership ID, and key profile details.

- The card can be downloaded and printed directly from the browser.
- To generate cards in bulk for all members, use the **Generate Membership Card** button on the [Members list](/docs/modules/user-management/members) toolbar.

---

### Exit Member

Marks the member as **exited** — indicating they have left the congregation (e.g., transferred, relocated, or deceased).

- Requires confirmation before applying.
- Exited members are **retained** in the system for historical record-keeping.
- They are excluded from active lists, communications, and attendance.

<Note type="warning">
  This action requires confirmation. Use **Deactivate** instead if the member's status is temporary.
</Note>
