# Export Members

The **Export** feature lets you download the full member list from ChurchCMS as a file, useful for reporting, data backups, or migrating data to external tools.

## How to Export

1. Go to **Users → Members**.
2. Click the **Export** button in the toolbar.
3. The file will be generated and downloaded automatically to your browser's default download location.

---

## Use Cases

| Use Case | Description |
|---|---|
| Reporting | Share member data with church leadership or committees |
| Backup | Keep an offline copy of member records |
| External tools | Import into mailing platforms, spreadsheets, or other CMS tools |
| Data audit | Review member information outside the system |

---

## Notes

<Note type="info">
  The export includes all **active members** at the time of export. Deactivated or exited members may not be included depending on system settings.
</Note>

<Note type="warning">
  Exported files contain **personally identifiable information (PII)** such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, and dates of birth. Handle exported files securely and in accordance with your church's data privacy policy.
</Note>
