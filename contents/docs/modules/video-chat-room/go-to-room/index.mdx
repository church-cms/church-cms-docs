## Step-by-Step Procedure

<Step>
  <StepItem title="Open Video Chat Room">
    From the dashboard, navigate to **Video Chat Room**.
  </StepItem>
  <StepItem title="Find the room">
    Locate the video chat room you want to enter.
    
 ![Search List](/modules/video-chat-room/search.png)
  </StepItem>
  <StepItem title="Click Go to Room">
    Click the **Go to Room** button on the room.

    ![Go to Room Button](/modules/video-chat-room/go-to-room-button.png)
  </StepItem>
  <StepItem title="Start communication">
    You will enter the video chat room. Start video communication with the invited members.
  </StepItem>
</Step>

## Outcome

- You will be connected to the video chat room.
- Invited members in the room can communicate in real-time.

<Note type="info">
  Video communication is hosted on a secure cloud server, ensuring safe and reliable interaction.
</Note>
