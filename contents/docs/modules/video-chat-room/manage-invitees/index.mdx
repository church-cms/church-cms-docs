---
title: How to Manage Invitees
description: Guide to adding or removing members from a video chat room in ChurchCMS.
---

## Step-by-Step Procedure

<Step>
  <StepItem title="Open Video Chat Room">
    From the dashboard, navigate to **Video Chat Room**.
  </StepItem>
  <StepItem title="Find the room">
    Locate the video chat room whose invitees you want to manage.

     ![Search List](/modules/video-chat-room/search.png)
  </StepItem>
  <StepItem title="Click Manage Invitees">
    Click the **Manage Invitees** button on the room.

    ![Manage Invitees Button](/modules/video-chat-room/manage-invitees-button.png)
  </StepItem>
  <StepItem title="Add or remove members">
    Add or remove members from the room as required.
  </StepItem>
  <StepItem title="Save changes">
    Click **Save** to apply the changes.
  </StepItem>
</Step>

## Outcome

- The invitee list for the room will be updated.
- Added members can join the room; removed members will lose access.
