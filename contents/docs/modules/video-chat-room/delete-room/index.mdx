---
title: How to Delete a Video Chat Room
description: Guide to removing a video chat room that is no longer required.
---

## Step-by-Step Procedure

<Step>
  <StepItem title="Open Video Chat Room">
    From the dashboard, navigate to **Video Chat Room**.
  </StepItem>
  <StepItem title="Find the room">
    Locate the video chat room you want to delete. Use the **Search** bar if needed to find it quickly.

    ![Search List](/modules/video-chat-room/search.png)
  </StepItem>
  <StepItem title="Click Delete">
    Click the **Delete** button on the room.

    ![Delete Room Button](/modules/video-chat-room/delete-room-button.png)
  </StepItem>
  <StepItem title="Confirm the action">
    Confirm the action to complete the deletion.
  </StepItem>
</Step>

## Outcome

- The video chat room will be removed permanently.
- Invited members will no longer have access to the room.

<Note type="warning">
  This action cannot be undone. Ensure the room is no longer needed before deleting it.
</Note>
