---
title: How to Edit a Video Chat Room
description: Guide to modifying an existing video chat room's title, description, and invited members.
---

## Step-by-Step Procedure

<Step>
  <StepItem title="Open Video Chat Room">
    From the dashboard, navigate to **Video Chat Room**.
  </StepItem>
  <StepItem title="Find the room">
    Locate the video chat room you want to edit. Use the **Search** bar if needed to find it quickly.

     ![Search List](/modules/video-chat-room/search.png)
  </StepItem>
  <StepItem title="Click Edit">
    Click the **Edit** button on the room.

    ![Edit Room Button](/modules/video-chat-room/edit-room-button.png)
  </StepItem>
  <StepItem title="Modify the details">
    Update the required fields:
    - **Title**
    - **Description**
    - **Invited Members**
  </StepItem>
  <StepItem title="Save changes">
    Click **Save** to apply the changes.
  </StepItem>
</Step>

## Outcome

- The video chat room details will be updated successfully.
- Changes to invited members take effect immediately.
