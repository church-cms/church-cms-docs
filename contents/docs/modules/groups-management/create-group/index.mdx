## Step-by-Step Procedure

<Step>
  <StepItem title="Click New Group">
    Click the **New Group** button.

    ![New Group Button](/modules/groups/new-group.png)
  </StepItem>
  <StepItem title="Group creation form opens">
    A new group creation form will open.
  </StepItem>
  <StepItem title="Enter the required details">
    Enter the required details:
    - **Select Category**
    - **Select Group Type**
    - **Group Name**
    - **Description**
    - **Upload Cover Image**

    ![Create Group Form](/modules/groups/create-group-form.png)
  </StepItem>
  <StepItem title="Upload">
    Click **Upload**.
  </StepItem>
</Step>
![Group Created successfully](/modules/groups/group-created-success.png)

## Outcome

- The group will be created successfully.
- It will be listed in the **Groups module**.

![Group Created](/modules/groups/group-created.png)
