# Delete Group

<Step>
  <StepItem title="Click Delete Group">
    Click **Delete Group**.

    ![Delete Group Button](/modules/groups/delete-group-button.png)
  </StepItem>
  <StepItem title="Confirm the action">
    Confirm the action.

    ![Delete Confirmation](/modules/groups/delete-group-confirmation.png)
  </StepItem>
  <StepItem title="Group deleted">
    The group will be deleted successfully.
  </StepItem>
</Step>

<Note type="warning" title="Notes">
  - Ensure correct member roles and permissions are assigned.
  - Deleting a group will remove all associated data and cannot be undone.
</Note>
