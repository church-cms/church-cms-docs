# How to Delete a Gallery

<Step>
  <StepItem title="Navigate to Gallery">
    Navigate to the **Gallery** section.
  </StepItem>
  <StepItem title="Locate the album">
    Locate the required album.
  </StepItem>
  <StepItem title="Click Delete icon">
    Click the **Delete (X) icon** on the album.

    ![Delete Gallery](/modules/gallery/delete-gallery.png)
  </StepItem>
  <StepItem title="Confirmation message">
    A confirmation message will appear.
  </StepItem>
  <StepItem title="Confirm deletion">
    Click **OK** to confirm deletion.
  </StepItem>
</Step>

## Outcome

- The gallery album and all images within it will be deleted permanently.

<Note type="warning" title="Notes">
  - Deleting a gallery will remove all associated images and cannot be undone.
  - Ensure correct selection before deleting any album.
</Note>
