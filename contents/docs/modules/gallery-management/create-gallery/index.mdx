## Step-by-Step Procedure

<Step>
  <StepItem title="Login to the system">
    Login to the system using **Admin credentials**.
  </StepItem>
  <StepItem title="Open Gallery">
    From the main menu, click **Gallery**.

    ![Gallery Menu](/modules/gallery/gallery-menu.png)
  </StepItem>
  <StepItem title="Click New Album">
    Click the **New Album** button.

    ![New Album Button](/modules/gallery/new-album.png)
  </StepItem>
  <StepItem title="Enter the required details">
    Enter the required details:
    - **Gallery Name**
    - **Cover Image**

    ![Create Album Form](/modules/gallery/create-album-form.png)
  </StepItem>
  <StepItem title="Submit the form">
    Click **Submit**.
  </StepItem>
</Step>

## Outcome

- A new gallery album will be created successfully.
- The album will be listed in the Gallery section.
